Terms of Service

Payment & Ownership

Deposit

A 40% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your spot. Work and design will ONLY START once this deposit is cleared.

Ownership

Full ownership transfers to the commissioner only upon final payment.

Abandonment

If there is no payment activity for 30 days after completion, the fursuit is considered abandoned. TropicalFrostStudios retains the suit and the deposit, and reserves the right to resell the item.

Payment Methods

    Method A (Standard):
  • 40% initial deposit; Another 30% after base completion; Final 30% before shipping.
    • Method B (Installments):
  • After the 40% deposit, remaining payments are split into 20% or 30% increments depending on complexity.
    • Note: Choosing smaller payment increments (20%) may result in a longer production timeline.

Copyright & Reselling

Customer Artwork

Any artwork provided by the commissioner for printing (e.g., on a mug or canvas) remains the sole property of the original artist/commissioner.

Usage

TropicalFrostStudios acts only as a printing service for these items. We will not store your digital files long-term, and we will never use your artwork for commercial purposes or resale.

Reselling

The commissioner has the right to sell their TropicalFrostStudios fursuit provided that the Character Rights (If had one) and Certificate of Authenticity are passed down to the new owner.

Returns, Refunds & Warranty

Warranty

We offer a 6-month repair guarantee. If any part falls apart on its own (maker error), return it to us and we will fix it for free, including shipping costs.

Timeline for Simple/Partial

Completion usually takes 2–3 months. If production exceeds 5 months, a full refund is available.

Timeline for Complex

Completion takes an additional 1–2 months. If production exceeds 8 months, a full refund is available.

Affiliate Program

Help us grow and get rewarded!

If you refer a client to us:

  • Option 1: Receive 10% of their total commission price in cash (paid after they secure their commission).
  • Option 2: Receive 20% of their commission value as a discount on your next fursuit.

Example: If a friend buys a $2,000 suit, you get $200 cash OR $400 off your next order!
